# Court Lists App - User Guide

A mobile app for viewing Irish court listings and collaborating with colleagues through comments and notifications.

---

## Getting Started

### Installation

**Android:**
1. Download the APK file to your phone
2. Open the file and tap "Install" (you may need to enable "Install from unknown sources" in Settings)
3. Open the app once installed

**iOS:**
1. Install via TestFlight or from the App Store (when available)
2. Open the app

### Signing In

1. Tap **Sign in with Google**
2. Select your Google account
3. If your account hasn't been approved yet, you'll see an "Approval Pending" screen - contact an administrator

---

## Viewing Court Lists

### Selecting a Date

**Android:**
- Tap the date at the top to open a calendar
- Navigate months with the left/right arrows
- Tap a date to select it
- Tap "All Dates" to clear the date filter

**iOS:**
- Tap the date picker at the top
- Select your desired date
- Use "All Dates" to clear the filter

### Filtering by Venue

1. Tap the venue dropdown (shows "All Venues" by default)
2. Select a specific venue to filter the list
3. Select "All Venues" to clear the filter

### Understanding the List View

Each court list shows:
- **Name** - The list name/title
- **Venue** - Court location
- **Type** - Type of court list
- **Updated** - When the list was last modified

Tap any list to view its cases.

---

## Viewing Cases

### The Case List

When you open a court list, you'll see:
- **List Notes** row at the top (for list-level comments)
- **Headers** - Collapsible section headers (tap to expand/collapse)
- **Cases** - Individual case entries

Each case shows:
- List number (position in the list)
- Case number (in bold)
- Case title/parties

### Live Update Counter

At the bottom of the screen, you'll see when the list was last updated. This updates in real-time when colleagues make changes.

---

## Marking Cases as Done

### To Mark a Case as Done

1. Tap anywhere on the case row
2. The case will be greyed out with strikethrough text
3. An "Undo" button appears on the right

### To Undo

- Tap the **Undo** button (circular arrow icon) to mark it as not done

All status changes sync in real-time with your colleagues.

---

## Comments

### Viewing Comments

1. Tap the **comment icon** on a case row
2. A sheet opens showing all comments for that case

### Adding a Comment

1. Open the comments sheet for a case
2. Type your message in the text field at the bottom
3. (Optional) Toggle **"Need help"** to mark as urgent - this shows a red hand icon
4. Tap the **Send** button

### Deleting Your Comments

- Your own comments show a **red trash icon** on the right
- Tap the trash icon to delete

### Sharing a Comment (Android only)

- Each comment has a **blue share icon**
- Tap to open the share sheet
- Select WhatsApp, Messages, Email, or any other app
- The shared text includes the case reference and comment content

---

## List Notes

List Notes are comments that apply to the entire list, not a specific case.

### Accessing List Notes

1. Open any court list
2. Tap the **"List Notes"** row at the top of the case list
3. Add comments that apply to the whole list

Use List Notes for general information like "Running 30 minutes late" or "Judge Smith presiding".

---

## Watch & Notifications

### Watching a Case

When you watch a case, you'll receive notifications when:
- Someone adds a comment
- Someone marks it as done
- Someone marks it as not done

**To watch a case:**
1. Tap the **bell icon** on the case row
2. The bell fills in to show it's being watched

**To stop watching:**
- Tap the bell icon again (it will show a slash through it)

### Watching a List

You can also watch List Notes for a court list:
1. Open the court list
2. Tap the bell icon on the **"List Notes"** row

### Viewing Notifications

1. Tap the **bell icon** in the top-right corner
2. The badge shows your unread count (99+ if more than 99)

### Notification Types

| Icon | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| Blue chat bubble | New comment |
| Green checkmark | Case marked as done |
| Orange/Red X | Case marked as not done |

### Managing Notifications

- **Tap a notification** to jump to that case
- **Mark all as read** - Use the checkmark icon at the top
- **Delete all** - Use the trash icon at the top

**iOS:** Swipe left to mark as read, swipe right to delete

## Signing Out

**Android:**
1. From the lists screen, tap the menu or your profile
2. Tap **Sign Out**

**iOS:**
1. Tap your profile/menu icon
2. Tap **Sign Out**

---

## Tips

1. **Use "Need help" sparingly** - The red hand icon draws attention, so reserve it for urgent matters

2. **Watch important cases** - You'll get notified of any activity without having to check manually

3. **Use List Notes** - Great for information that affects the whole list, not just one case

4. **Share to WhatsApp** (Android) - Quickly share case updates with colleagues who aren't using the app

5. **Pull to refresh** - Swipe down on any list to manually refresh the data

---

## Troubleshooting

### "No data" or empty lists
- Check your internet connection
- Try signing out and back in
- Make sure the date filter isn't set to a date with no listings

### Can't sign in
- Ensure you're using the correct Google account
- Check if your account has been approved by an administrator

### Comments not appearing
- Pull to refresh the case list
- Check your internet connection

### Notifications not working
- Ensure you've granted notification permissions to the app
- Check that you've watched the relevant cases

---

## Privacy & Data

- All data is synced through secure servers
- Comments are visible to all users of the app
- Your email is visible to other users when you comment
